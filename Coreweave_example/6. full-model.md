### 12. CF CHECK Historical Reconciliation

**User asked:** CF CHECK for FY2024-2025 is not equal to 0 — is this OK?

**AI investigation:**
- CF Ending Cash (includes restricted cash): FY2024 $2,035M, FY2025 $4,130M
- BS Cash (excludes restricted cash): FY2024 $1,398M, FY2025 $3,980M
- Gap = restricted cash: FY2024 $637M, FY2025 $150M

**Conclusion:** This is standard US GAAP treatment. CF Statement reconciles to total cash (incl. restricted), BS reports unrestricted cash only. Forecast years CHECK = 0 by construction (BS Cash ← CF Ending Cash link).

**Fix:** Updated BS Cash label (removed "(incl restricted)"), CF CHECK notes to explain the gap.
